About

CUhackit is the premier hackathon of South Carolina. We are passionate about providing the ultimate hackathon experience for hackers of all skill levels.

CUhackit lasts 36 hours total from January 25-27. While staying the whole 36 hours is not required, it is preferred.

Participants will mostly spend their time on coding projects, although there will be plenty of other activities like cup stacking and dance classes to keep everyone engaged (and awake).
