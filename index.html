About HelloWorld

We know hackathons can be a bit intimidating for those new to the community. Thatâ€™s why we created HelloWorld, a beginner-friendly hackathon where Clemson freshmen with little to no programming experience can be introduced to the power and fun of computer science. With our pre-event workshops and awesome mentors for each team, HelloWorld creates an inclusive and supportive environment that is open to all skill levels and majors.

This 12-hour event is open to all freshman at Clemson University. Whether you have some programming experience or not, we encourage you to apply!

The Plan

Registration Begins

8:00

Participants are able to start registering for the event. Breakfast will be served

Opening Ceremony

8:30

A speaker will introduce CUhackit and some of the team who has put in countless hours to make this event possible

Hacking Begins

9:00

On your marks, get set, GO!

Idea Jam Workshop

9:30

Writer's block can affect hackers too. This is an all-ideas-welcome forum with a Clemson faculty member to get the creative juices flowing

Intro to GitHub Workshop

10:30

Everybody's favorite version control system. GitHub will aid in your project development and team management. It is also a great backup service to protect your amazing work

Lunch

12:00

Lunch will be provided to all participants. Grab a plate and get your fill

Cup Stacking

2:00

A hackathon staple, compete to prove that your team can stack cups higher than the others. Rise among your peers, show what your team is made of

VR Demo

3:30

Clemson's very own Virtual Reality Club will be setting up stations for all participants to experience the amazing world of VR

Hacking Ends

6:00

It's time to let your keyboard cool down. You've worked hard to get here, it's finally over. Take a breather, you deserve it

Dinner

6:30

Dinner is provided to all participants. Take some time to relax after all of your hard work

Demo Judging

7:00

Present what you've made to Clemson Faculty and Graduate Students. They are all very excited to meet you and see what you've brought to the table

Closing Ceremony

8:00

It's time to send you on your way. We are so honored that you chose to spend your weekend with us and cannot wait to see you in the spring for CUhackit!
